MBM Admission Process 2026: Complete Guide, Dates & Registration

The MBM University Admission Process 2026 (conducted via REAP) is the premier gateway for aspiring engineers seeking admission into undergraduate B.Tech and B.E. programs at the prestigious MBM University, Jodhpur. Conducted by the Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG), Rajasthan as part of the state-wide counselling, this centralized mechanism streamlines the admission process into one of Rajasthan's oldest and most renowned engineering institutions.

If you are aiming to secure a seat in MBM University, Jodhpur, navigating the MBM Admission 2026 process efficiently is crucial. The counselling is primarily based on the merit of JEE Main 2026 scores and Class 12th board marks. In this definitive, comprehensive guide, we will break down every single aspect of the MBM 2026 counselling—from tentative dates and strict eligibility criteria to the nuanced steps of choice filling, seat allotment, and document verification.

Detailed Eligibility Criteria for MBM Admission 2026

Eligibility is the absolute foundation of your MBM admission application. If a candidate registers for the counselling but fails to meet the criteria mandated by CEG, AICTE, and MBM University, their candidacy will be immediately canceled at the document verification stage. The rules are divided into academic qualifications, age limits, and medical standards.

1. Academic Qualifications

Candidates must have passed their Class 10+2 (Intermediate) examination or equivalent from a recognized State or Central Board (such as CBSE, RBSE, CISCE, etc.).

  • Compulsory Subjects: The candidate must have studied Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects in Class 12th.
  • Optional Subjects: Along with Physics and Mathematics, the candidate must have studied at least one of the following subjects: Chemistry, Biotechnology, Biology, or Technical Vocational subjects.
  • Minimum Marks Required: General category candidates must have scored an aggregate of at least 45% marks in the above-mentioned subjects taken together. Reserved category candidates (SC/ST/Non-Creamy Layer OBC/Non-Creamy Layer MBC of Rajasthan state) are granted a relaxation and require a minimum of 40% aggregate marks.

2. Age Limit

Over the past few admission cycles, CEG Rajasthan has removed the strict upper age limit for applying to MBM University, aligning with national guidelines. However, candidates must possess a valid passing certificate and adhere to any specific age mandates set by individual participating institutions, if applicable.

3. Domicile Requirements

One of the most defining features of MBM admission is its priority structure. While candidates from all across India are absolutely welcome to apply, preference is given to the native candidates of Rajasthan who possess a valid state domicile certificate. MBM being a state government institution heavily prioritizes Rajasthan domiciled students for majority of its seats.

4. Medical Fitness

Every candidate allotted a seat through MBM Admission 2026 is required to produce a Medical Fitness Certificate at the time of reporting to the university. This certificate must be signed by a registered medical practitioner (Medical Officer or above) verifying that the candidate is medically fit to undergo a rigorous four-year engineering program.

Step-by-Step Registration Process

The application procedure for MBM Admission 2026 is entirely digital. No offline forms will be accepted. It is imperative that candidates fill in their details with utmost accuracy, particularly their JEE Main application numbers and Class 12 roll numbers, as the system fetches data automatically for verification.

Step 1: Application Fee Payment

Unlike many other exams where you pay at the end, the MBM admission process initiates with the fee payment. Candidates must visit the official portal and navigate to the "Fee Payment" link. You will be required to enter basic details (Name, Father's Name, Date of Birth, Category) and pay the non-refundable fee of INR 590/- (as per previous trends) via Net Banking, Credit Card, Debit Card, or E-Mitra Kiosk. Keep the 16-digit transaction ID safe; it is your key to unlocking the application form.

Step 2: Filling the Online Application Form

Using the transaction ID, log in to the portal. You will be prompted to enter your comprehensive personal details, academic history, and contact information. Here, you must carefully select your basis of admission:

  • Priority 1: On the basis of JEE Main 2026 Percentile/Rank.
  • Priority 2: On the basis of Class 12th Board Marks / Percentile.
  • Priority 3: Diploma in Engineering candidates (for regular first-year admission, distinct from LEEP).

Step 3: Uploading Documents

Candidates need to upload scanned copies of recent passport-size photographs and their signature in standard JPG/JPEG formats within the prescribed file size limit (usually 50KB to 100KB for photos, 20KB to 50KB for signatures). Some specific category certificates may also need to be uploaded.

Step 4: Submission & Printout

Review the entire application form thoroughly before final submission. Once locked, the data cannot be altered easily. Generate the final confirmation page and take at least two physical printouts. You do not need to send this printout to the CEG office, but it is strictly required during physical reporting at MBM University.

Merit List Preparation Formula

Because MBM admission is a non-examination-based counselling process, the Merit List acts as the ultimate determining factor for your branch allocation. The CEG prepares a unified State Merit List based strictly on the priority order mentioned below.

First Preference (JEE Main candidates): All seats will initially be offered to candidates applying on the basis of their JEE Main 2026 NTA scores. A distinct merit rank is generated based on the JEE Main AIR. This group enjoys the first right of refusal for all premium seats in government and top private colleges.

Second Preference (Class 12 Marks): If seats remain vacant after the JEE Main merit list is exhausted, or if a candidate did not appear for JEE Main, the merit is prepared on the basis of the percentile obtained in Class 12th board exams. Note: Percentile normalization formulas are often applied to bring candidates from CBSE, RBSE, and ICSE on a level playing field.

Counselling & Choice Filling Stages

The core of MBM Admission 2026 lies in its intricate choice filling and seat allotment algorithms. Once the application is completed and the merit list is published, the portal opens for choice filling. Read our detailed analysis on MBM Cutoffs to make informed decisions.

  • Choice Filling: You are allowed to select unlimited combinations of institutions and branches. It is highly advised to arrange your choices in strictly decreasing order of your preference. Place your desired MBM branches at the top.
  • Choice Locking: Once satisfied, candidates must lock their choices. If a candidate forgets to lock them, the system automatically locks the last saved choices at the deadline.
  • Seat Allotment: The algorithm matches your merit rank, category, and filled choices to allocate the best possible seat. Results are published online. You will see which institute and branch you have been awarded.
  • Upward Movement: If you are not satisfied with your allotted seat, you can apply for 'Upward Movement' (Upgradation). You must still report to the initially allotted institute and pay the fees to reserve your spot, but you opt to be considered for higher choices in the next round. If upgraded, your previous seat is automatically canceled.

TFWS and Reservation Policy

The Government of Rajasthan enforces a strict reservation policy for state-domiciled candidates in the MBM admission process. Candidates from Outside Rajasthan fall under the General Category regardless of their caste status in their home state.

Standard Reservation (Approximate Percentages): SC (16%), ST (12%), OBC-NCL (21%), MBC-NCL (5%), and EWS (10%). Furthermore, a horizontal reservation is provided for Persons with Disabilities (PwD), Ex-Servicemen, and Female candidates (usually 25% of seats across all categories are reserved for women).

Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme (TFWS): Up to 5% of supernumerary seats in all participating engineering colleges are reserved under the TFWS scheme for candidates whose total annual family income is strictly less than INR 8 Lakhs. Candidates securing a TFWS seat do not have to pay the basic tuition fee for the entire four years, though they must pay development, hostel, and examination fees. You must submit a valid, recent Income Certificate issued by a competent authority (Tehsildar/SDM) to claim this benefit.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can candidates from states outside Rajasthan apply for MBM Admission 2026?

Yes, candidates from across India can apply for MBM admission. However, 85% of seats at MBM University are reserved for Rajasthan domiciled students. Outside state candidates can compete for the remaining 15% quota, and they will be treated under the unreserved (General) category.

Is it mandatory to appear for JEE Main for MBM Admission?

No, it is not strictly mandatory. While the first priority and the top-tier university seats go to candidates with valid JEE Main 2026 scores, students can also apply based solely on their Class 12th board marks. The Class 12th merit list is executed after the JEE Main merit list is exhausted.

What happens if I miss the reporting deadline for the allotted branch/institute?

If you fail to report physically to MBM University (or your allotted institute) with all required documents and fee payment before the stated deadline, your allotted seat will be automatically canceled. You may be pushed out of the subsequent formal counselling rounds, though you might be eligible for spot rounds later.

How does the Upward Movement process work?

If you receive a seat but desire a branch or institute higher up on your preference list, you can apply for 'Upward Movement'. You must first report to the currently allotted branch/institute to secure your backup. If a higher-preference seat becomes vacant during subsequent rounds, you will be upgraded, and your previous seat will be canceled.
