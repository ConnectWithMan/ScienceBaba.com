REAP Cutoff 2025: Expected Ranks & Previous Year Trends

The Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP) 2025 Cutoff determines the minimum merit rank required for admission into various B.Tech and B.E. programs across government and private engineering institutions in Rajasthan. Conducted by the Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG), the cutoff is released as opening and closing ranks after each round of seat allotment.

Understanding the REAP cutoff trends is crucial for candidates participating in the counselling process. It helps students gauge their admission chances in top colleges like MBM University Jodhpur, CTAE Udaipur, and RTU Kota based on their JEE Main scores or Class 12th percentile. In this comprehensive guide, we will analyze the expected cutoffs, factors affecting them, and previous year trends.

How to Check REAP Cutoff 2025?

Once released, candidates can check the official REAP cutoff list by following these simple steps:

Step 1: Visit the Official Website

Go to the official REAP 2025 portal governed by CEG Rajasthan.

Step 2: Navigate to Downloads/Notifications

Look for the 'Seat Allotment Cutoff' or 'Opening and Closing Ranks' link in the latest notifications or downloads section of the homepage.

Step 3: Select the Round

Click on the PDF link for the respective counselling round (e.g., Round 1, Round 2, or TFWS round). The board publishes separate PDFs for different rounds and categories.

Step 4: Search for Your Dream College

Open the PDF and use the search function (Ctrl+F or Cmd+F) to find your desired institute and branch to check the category-wise closing ranks. Compare it with your state merit rank.

Factors Affecting REAP 2025 Cutoff

The cutoff ranks for REAP fluctuate every year based on several dynamic factors. Understanding these can help you better predict your chances:

  • Number of Applicants: An increase in the total number of students applying for REAP generally leads to higher cutoffs and stiffer competition.
  • Difficulty Level of JEE Main: Since REAP prioritizes JEE Main scores, the overall difficulty and scoring trends of the JEE Main exam directly impact the REAP merit list.
  • Availability of Seats: The total number of sanctioned seats in participating colleges. The introduction of new branches or increased intake can shift cutoff trends.
  • Reservation Categories: Cutoffs vary significantly across different categories (General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS, etc.) and domicile statuses (Rajasthan state candidates vs. Out of State).
  • Branch Popularity: The popularity of certain branches (like Computer Science Engineering and IT) keeps their cutoffs consistently high year over year compared to core branches.

Types of Merit Lists for REAP Cutoff

CEG Rajasthan prepares separate merit lists, which eventually dictate the cutoffs for different applicant groups:

  • JEE Main Candidates: First priority is given to candidates with valid JEE Main scores. The cutoff for top colleges usually closes entirely within this list.
  • Class 12th Marks: Seats left vacant after exhausting the JEE Main merit list are filled based on Class 12th board percentiles.
  • TFWS Cutoff: A separate, usually much higher cutoff is released for candidates applying under the Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme (TFWS).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) on REAP Cutoff

Will the REAP Cutoff be based on JEE Main or Class 12th marks?

First preference is given to JEE Main scores. If seats remain vacant, they are allotted based on Class 12th percentiles. Separate cutoffs are declared for both entry methods.

Is there a different cutoff for Out-of-State candidates?

Yes, 15% of the total seats in private and government-aided colleges are reserved for candidates outside Rajasthan, and they have a separate All India merit list and cutoff.

Does the cutoff drop in subsequent counselling rounds?

Generally, yes. The closing rank usually drops in Round 2 (Upward Movement) and significantly in the Spot/Special rounds as students withdraw to join other national institutes like NITs or IITs.

Where can I check the official REAP opening and closing ranks?

The official cutoff PDFs are published on the CEG Rajasthan's REAP portal after every allotment round in the notifications section.
