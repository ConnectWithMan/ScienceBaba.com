REAP Application Form 2026

The REAP application form for the 2026 academic session will be released by the Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG), Rajasthan. Candidates aspiring for B.Tech, B.E., and B.Arch admissions in government and private colleges across Rajasthan must complete their registration online. You can access and fill out the official REAP application form directly through the REAP 2026 official portal.

Who Can Fill the REAP Application Form? (Eligibility)

Before proceeding to the registration portal, candidates must ensure they meet the eligibility criteria to fill the REAP application form. Submitting an application without meeting these requirements will lead to disqualification.

  • Academic Qualification: Candidates must have passed their 10+2 (Class 12th) examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects, along with one optional subject (Chemistry, Biology, Biotechnology, or Technical Vocational subject).
  • Minimum Marks: A minimum of 45% marks (40% for reserved categories of Rajasthan) in the qualifying examination is mandatory.
  • Entrance Exam: While candidates with a valid JEE Main 2026 scorecard are given first preference during seat allotment, non-JEE candidates can also submit the REAP application form based on their Class 12th board marks.

How to Fill the REAP 2026 Application Form?

Candidates must follow a systematic process to successfully submit their application. Here are the required steps:

  • Step 1: Online Registration: Visit the official website and register using basic details like name, mobile number, and email ID to generate login credentials.
  • Step 2: Fee Payment: Pay the non-refundable REAP application fee of INR 295/- via Netbanking, UPI, or Credit/Debit card.
  • Step 3: Fill Application Details: Log in to complete your personal profile, academic records (10th, 12th, or JEE Main scores), and category information.
  • Step 4: Upload Documents: Upload scanned copies of your recent photograph, signature, mark sheets, and category certificates (if applicable).
  • Step 5: Choice Filling & Locking: Select your preferred engineering colleges and branches. Lock the choices before the final deadline to confirm your form submission.

Documents Required for Application

Before starting the REAP application form, keep the following documents scanned and ready for upload:

  • Valid JEE Main 2026 Scorecard (if applying through JEE quota)
  • Class 10th and 12th Marksheets
  • Domicile Certificate (for Rajasthan residents)
  • Caste/Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS)
  • Income Certificate (if applying under TFWS)
  • Medical Fitness Certificate

Common Mistakes to Avoid in the REAP Application Form

To ensure your admission process goes smoothly, avoid these common errors while submitting your REAP application form:

  • Incorrect Category Selection: Ensure you select the correct reservation category (General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS). Changing this after the REAP application form is submitted is usually not permitted.
  • Blurry Document Uploads: Upload clear, high-resolution scans of your documents. Unreadable documents can lead to the rejection of your REAP application form during online verification.
  • Not Locking Choices: Merely filling the form is not enough. Candidates must explicitly lock their college and branch choices before the final deadline.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: When will the REAP application form 2026 be released?
A: The online application form is tentatively expected to be available from the last week of May 2026 on the official website.

Q: What is the REAP 2026 application fee?
A: The registration fee for all candidates is generally INR 295/-. This must be paid online before proceeding to fill out the detailed form.

Q: Can I edit the REAP application form after final submission?
A: Typically, no correction window is provided once the final submission and choice locking are done. Candidates are advised to carefully verify all details before submitting.
