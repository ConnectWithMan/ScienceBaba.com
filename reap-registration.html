REAP 2026 Registration Date & Application Form

Updated: March 2026

The Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG), Rajasthan, will soon announce the official dates for the Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP) 2026. Registering for REAP is the first and most crucial step for candidates seeking admission to B.E., B.Tech, and B.Arch programs in various government and private technical colleges across Rajasthan.

REAP 2026 Registration Dates (Tentative)

It is highly recommended that candidates keep a close eye on the application deadlines. Below is the expected schedule for the REAP 2026 registration process:

Registration Event Expected Date
Release of REAP 2026 Application Form Last week of May 2026
Last Date to Pay Registration Fee Last week of July 2026
Last Date to Submit Application Form Last week of July 2026
Last Date for Choice Filling & Locking First week of August 2026

Registration Fee Details

Candidates must pay a non-refundable application fee to successfully register for REAP 2026. The fee details are generally as follows:

  • Registration Fee: Rs. 295/- (Rs. 250 + 18% GST)
  • Payment Mode: The fee can only be paid online via Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card, or UPI.
  • Note: The application form will not be considered complete until the fee is successfully processed.

How to Apply for REAP 2026?

The entire registration process is conducted online. Follow these steps to complete your REAP application:

Step 1: Fee Payment

Visit the official REAP portal and click on the registration link. You will first need to pay the online registration fee of Rs. 295/- to generate your unique Transaction ID.

Step 2: Fill the Application Form

Using the Transaction ID, log in to the portal and fill out your personal details, academic qualifications (Class 12th marks or JEE Main details), and contact information.

Step 3: Upload Documents

Upload scanned copies of your recent passport-sized photograph and signature in the prescribed format and size (usually JPEG/JPG).

Step 4: Submit & Print

Review all entered details carefully. Once confirmed, submit the application and take a printout of the final confirmation page for future reference during document verification.

Conclusion

Missing the REAP 2026 registration deadline means missing the chance to participate in the entire counselling process. Ensure you have your documents, photographs, and payment methods ready by the last week of May so you can register smoothly without any last-minute hassle.

Frequently Asked Questions about Registration

When will the REAP 2026 registration start?

The REAP 2026 registration process is tentatively expected to begin in the last week of May 2026. The official notification will confirm the exact date.

What is the last date to apply for REAP 2026?

The last date to submit the online application form and pay the registration fee is generally in the last week of July 2026.

Can I fill out the REAP application form offline?

No, the REAP application process is completely online. No physical forms will be accepted by the Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG).

What is the registration fee for REAP 2026?

The registration fee for REAP 2026 is Rs. 295/- (Rs. 250 + 18% GST), which is strictly non-refundable and must be paid online.

Can I edit my application form after submitting it?

Generally, REAP does not provide an application correction window. It is crucial to double-check all information before final submission to avoid disqualification.
