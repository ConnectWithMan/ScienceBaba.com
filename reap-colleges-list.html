REAP 2026 Participating Colleges: REAP Colleges List

The Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP) 2026 opens doors to numerous prestigious engineering colleges across the state. Choosing the right institution is a pivotal step in your academic journey and future career. Through REAP, candidates can secure admission into B.Tech, B.E., and B.Arch programs offered by Government-aided institutions, State Universities, and top Private Engineering Colleges in Rajasthan.

Since the counselling is primarily based on the merit of JEE Main 2026 scores and Class 12th board marks, knowing which colleges to prioritize during the choice-filling phase is highly critical. In this comprehensive guide, we have compiled a detailed list of the top participating colleges for REAP 2026 to help you make an informed decision.

State University Departments

Several state universities in Rajasthan have their own technical departments that accept students through the REAP counselling process. These often provide a vibrant university campus life.

  • University Departments, Rajasthan Technical University (RTU), Kota: Being the primary technical university in the state, RTU's main campus is highly preferred for various core branches.
  • University College of Engineering and Technology (UCET), Bikaner: Constituent college of Bikaner Technical University (BTU).
  • College of Engineering and Technology, Bikaner: Another excellent campus for technical education in the northern Rajasthan region.

Factors to Consider When Choosing a College

During the choice-filling stage of REAP 2026, you will be allowed to fill multiple preferences. Here are some critical factors to evaluate before locking your choices:

  • Placement Records: Review the past 3-4 years of placement data. Look for average packages, highest packages, and the core companies visiting the campus.
  • Location & Accessibility: Consider the city where the college is located. Cities like Jaipur, Jodhpur, and Udaipur often offer better internship and networking opportunities.
  • Accreditation & Affiliation: Ensure the college is approved by AICTE and affiliated with RTU Kota or BTU Bikaner. NBA accredited branches are a massive plus.
  • Infrastructure & Labs: Engineering is practical. Check if the college has up-to-date laboratories, libraries, and Wi-Fi enabled campuses.
  • Fee Structure vs ROI: Evaluate if the total cost of the 4-year degree aligns with the potential return on investment (placements).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Which are the top government engineering colleges participating in REAP?

Some of the most prestigious government colleges under REAP include MBM University (Jodhpur), CTAE (Udaipur), and Engineering College Ajmer (ECA).

Do private colleges participate in REAP counselling?

Yes, numerous top private engineering colleges in Rajasthan, such as SKIT Jaipur, JECRC, and Poornima College, participate in REAP and allocate seats through this centralized process.

Can I get direct admission in these colleges without REAP?

Many private colleges have a specific management quota (usually 15%) for direct admissions. However, for government seats and state-quota in private colleges, applying through REAP is mandatory.

Are university departments included in REAP?

Yes, technical university departments like those at RTU Kota and BTU Bikaner are a major part of the REAP counselling process.
