IPL Ticket Booking 2026: The Ultimate Guide

Updated on March 28, 2026 • 15 min read

The Indian Premier League (IPL) returns for its 19th season in 2026, promising another summer of high-octane cricketing action. As the world's premier T20 league, the demand for tickets is unprecedented. Whether you want to witness the "El Clásico" of cricket between Mumbai Indians and Chennai Super Kings or support your local franchise, securing a seat in the stadium requires planning and speed.

This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about IPL 2026 ticket booking, including official ticketing partners, price ranges, step-by-step booking procedures, and tips to grab tickets for high-demand matches.

Official IPL Ticket Booking Platforms

For IPL 2026, the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) and the franchises have partnered with major ticketing platforms. It is crucial to book only through these official channels to avoid scams and counterfeit tickets.

  • BookMyShow: The primary ticketing partner for the majority of the teams, including Mumbai Indians, Rajasthan Royals, Kolkata Knight Riders, and Gujarat Titans.
  • Paytm Insider: The official partner for Chennai Super Kings, Delhi Capitals, Punjab Kings, and Sunrisers Hyderabad.
  • Official Team Websites: Many franchises like Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) offer early access or exclusive membership quotas directly through their official apps and websites.

Step-by-Step Guide to Book IPL Tickets Online

Booking tickets online is the most convenient way to secure your spot. Follow these steps to ensure a smooth experience:

  1. Create an Account: Before tickets go live, ensure you have an active account on BookMyShow or Paytm Insider with your payment details saved. This saves valuable time during the rush.
  2. Select Your City/Team: Navigate to the "Sports" or "IPL 2026" section on the app or website. Select the match you wish to attend.
  3. Choose Your Stand: The stadium layout will be displayed. Stands are usually color-coded by price. Click on a block to see available seats.
  4. Select Seats: You can typically select up to 4 tickets per user for high-demand games. Choose your seats and proceed to checkout.
  5. Payment: Complete the payment using UPI, Credit/Debit Card, or Net Banking within the time limit (usually 5-10 minutes).
  6. Delivery: You will receive an M-ticket via email/SMS. Note that for some venues, you must exchange this M-ticket for a physical entry pass at the stadium box office before the match.

Team-Wise Ticket Booking Details (2026)

Each franchise manages its own ticket sales timeline. Here is a breakdown of what to expect for each team:

1. Chennai Super Kings (CSK)

Tickets for matches at the M.A. Chidambaram Stadium (Chepauk) are notoriously difficult to get. Sales usually open on Paytm Insider about 2 weeks before the first home game. CSK also offers a membership program that grants early access to tickets.

2. Mumbai Indians (MI)

Matches at the Wankhede Stadium are sold via BookMyShow. MI offers a "Slice" card pre-sale for holders of specific credit cards. The "Gold" and "Silver" memberships on the MI website also provide early booking windows.

3. Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB)

The M. Chinnaswamy Stadium atmosphere is electric. RCB tickets are often sold exclusively on the RCB website and app for the first 48 hours before being listed on general platforms. Prices here tend to be slightly higher due to the premium city location.

4. Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR)

Eden Gardens has a massive capacity, making it slightly easier to get general tickets. BookMyShow is the usual partner. KKR offers excellent hospitality packages for corporate groups.

5. Gujarat Titans (GT)

Playing at the Narendra Modi Stadium, the world's largest cricket stadium, GT tickets are generally available due to the huge seating capacity (132,000). Prices are very affordable, starting as low as ₹499 for upper tiers.

Offline Ticket Booking

While online booking is preferred, a small percentage of tickets are sold offline at stadium box offices. This usually happens 2-3 days before the match.

  • Pros: No internet handling fees; getting physical tickets instantly.
  • Cons: Long queues; high risk of "Sold Out" signs; black market risks (avoid buying from touts).

Tips to Secure Tickets for High-Voltage Matches

Matches like MI vs CSK or RCB vs CSK sell out in minutes. Here are pro tips to improve your chances:

  • Join Fan Clubs: Official fan clubs (like the Whistle Podu Army or Bharat Army) often get allocation codes.
  • Use Multiple Devices: When the queue opens online, log in from your phone and laptop simultaneously to increase your chances of getting through the virtual waiting room.
  • Check for "Resale" Tickets: Some platforms allow fans to resell tickets officially if they cannot attend. Keep checking closer to the match date.

Stadium Entry Guidelines & Rules

Once you have your ticket, ensure you follow the stadium rules for a hassle-free entry:

  • Physical Tickets: Even if you booked online, some stadiums (like Mohali or Jaipur) require you to redeem the online receipt for a physical ticket at designated kiosks. Check your email instructions carefully.
  • Prohibited Items: Do not carry power banks, coins, headphones, cameras, bottles, or huge bags. These are strictly banned and locker facilities are often overcrowded.
  • Re-entry: Once you exit the stadium gates, re-entry is generally not permitted.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I cancel my IPL ticket?

No, IPL tickets are generally non-refundable and non-cancellable unless the match is washed out without a single ball being bowled.

Is entry free for children?

Rules vary by stadium, but typically children above the age of 2 or 3 years require a full ticket. Infants may be allowed free entry but without a seat.

When do IPL 2026 tickets go on sale?

Tickets typically go on sale 2-3 weeks before the tournament starts. For the later phases (playoffs and finals), tickets are released separately in batches.

Conclusion: The IPL 2026 season is set to be bigger than ever. By planning ahead and using the official booking partners outlined in this guide, you can ensure you are part of the history being made on the pitch. Keep your apps updated and your notifications on!
