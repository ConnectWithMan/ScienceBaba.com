Monalisa Bhosle Marries Farman Khan

Published on March 25, 2026 • 5 min read

In what is being hailed as the most spectacular wedding of 2026, television sensation Monalisa Bhosle has tied the knot with her longtime beau, Farman Khan. The grand ceremony took place at a heritage palace in Rajasthan, attended by the crème de la crème of the entertainment industry.

The Grand Ceremony

The festivities spanned three days of opulent celebrations, blending traditional customs with modern glamour. The main ceremony was a sight to behold, with the venue decked in pastel florals and cascading lights that created a fairytale ambiance.

The Vows

Friends and family were moved to tears as the couple exchanged vows. Farman Khan, looking dashing in an ivory sherwani, waited eagerly as Monalisa walked down the aisle, radiating elegance in a custom-designed lehenga that reportedly took six months to craft.

Star-Studded Guest List

The wedding saw a massive turnout from the television and film fraternity. Prominent stars were seen dancing to popular beats during the Sangeet night, making it an evening to remember.

The Love Story

Monalisa and Farman's journey began on the sets of their hit show three years ago. What started as an on-screen friendship blossomed into a real-life romance that has captured the hearts of fans worldwide. Their wedding marks the beginning of a new chapter in their lives.

The Grand Mumbai Reception

Following the intimate Udaipur wedding, the couple hosted a magnificent reception at The Taj Lands End in Mumbai last night. The event was a star-studded affair attended by Bollywood A-listers. Monalisa dazzled in a golden sequined gown by Falguni Shane Peacock, while Farman looked sharp in a classic black tuxedo.

Upcoming Projects

Despite the grand celebrations, the couple isn't taking a long break. Sources confirm that Farman will fly to London next week to begin filming for his debut web series, while Monalisa will resume her shoot for 'Dil Diya Gallan' in Mumbai.

Inside the Sangeet Night

Sources reveal that the Sangeet night was a high-energy affair. Farman surprised Monalisa with a choreographed performance to a medley of her favorite romantic tracks. The bride joined in later, and the duo set the stage on fire with their chemistry.

Honeymoon Plans

While the couple has remained tight-lipped about their honeymoon destination, insiders suggest they might be heading to the Swiss Alps for a romantic getaway before returning to their shooting schedules.
